Data Samiksha

A project expo where participants tackle real-world Smart India Hackathon (SIH) problem statements using Data Science and Machine Learning. The event focuses on applying analytical and technical skills to design innovative, data-driven solutions.

Data Samiksha - Project Expo Event

Guidelines

  • Each team can have 4 members.
  • Participants should select an SIH-style problem statement and develop a data-driven solution using Data Science or Machine Learning.
  • Teams must present their project, approach, and outcomes through a prototype.
  • Projects will be judged based on innovation, problem-solving, and presentation.
  • All ideas should be original and created by the participants.

Outcome

Participants gain hands-on experience, enhance teamwork and problem-solving skills, and learn to apply AI and ML concepts to real challenges bridging the gap between academics and industry innovation.
