Data Samiksha - Project Expo Event

Description

Participants will tackle real-world Smart India Hackathon (SIH) problem statements using Data Science and Machine Learning. The event focuses on applying analytical and technical skills to design innovative, data-driven solutions and prototypes.

Guidelines

Teams will present their projects demonstrating problem understanding, data pipeline, modeling choices, evaluation, and an implementation or prototype. Presentations should include a clear problem statement, approach, results, and potential impact. Judging will emphasize technical merit, innovation, reproducibility, and presentation quality.

Outcome

Participants gain hands-on experience, enhance teamwork and problem-solving skills, and learn to apply AI and ML concepts to real challenges — bridging the gap between academics and industry innovation.
