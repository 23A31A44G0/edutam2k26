Data Pravachan - Data Storytelling Event

Description

It encourages creativity, analytical thinking, and effective communication—empowering budding data scientists to convey impactful stories that inspire decisions and highlight the true power of data.

Guidelines

Participants will prepare a short data story using provided or self-sourced datasets and present it combining visuals and narrative. Judging will be based on clarity, insightfulness, visualization quality, storytelling, and practical relevance.

Outcome

Participants will develop skills in presenting data through impactful stories and visuals. They will learn to communicate insights effectively, bridging the gap between analysis and understanding.
