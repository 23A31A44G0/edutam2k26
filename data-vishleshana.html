Data Vishleshana - Technical Quiz Event

Guidelines

Participants will compete in timed quiz rounds covering topics from data preprocessing, model selection, evaluation metrics, algorithms, probability & statistics, and applied ML scenarios. Rounds may include multiple-choice questions, reasoning problems, and algorithmic/data interpretation tasks.

Outcome

This event enhances problem-solving and decision-making abilities. It encourages teamwork and quick thinking, helping students strengthen their technical foundation and apply conceptual knowledge effectively in real-world scenarios.
